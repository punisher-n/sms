Why Auto Annotaion?

Everyone who worked with an object detection problem knows how is boring and spends time the process to annotate images. Annotate itself its pretty simple, we just need to mark the object location and tells it class. There is no problem to do it for some images, but when we have hundreds or even thousands of data in our dataset, what is normal with deep learning, it turns in a bottleneck for our work.
